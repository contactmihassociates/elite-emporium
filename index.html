❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers
How do I place an order?

Browse products, click Add to Cart, then go to the cart and fill in your delivery details. Click Place Order via WhatsApp — your full order will open in WhatsApp pre-filled. Just send it to us and we'll confirm within minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Bank Transfer, and Cash via WhatsApp confirmation. We'll share UPI / bank details after you place your order.

How long does delivery take?

3–7 business days anywhere in India. Free delivery on orders above ₹499 (₹60 below that). We ship from Kayalpattinam, Tamil Nadu, typically the same day if you order before 4 PM IST.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes — within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, with tags intact. Refunds are issued as store credit or bank transfer within 3–5 business days. Worn clothing and opened cosmetics aren't eligible.

Do you provide a GST invoice?

Yes — GST invoice is available on every order. Just request it on WhatsApp when placing the order. Our GST: 33DWGPN3169G1ZF.

How can I track my order?

Once dispatched, we share a courier tracking link directly on WhatsApp. You can also see your order status in My Orders — Ordered → Confirmed → Shipped → Delivered.

Are the products genuine?

Yes. Elite Emporium is a GST-registered, Udyam-certified micro-enterprise. We curate every product personally. If you receive anything not as described, we'll refund or replace — no questions asked.

Do you ship outside India?

Currently we ship only within India. For international enquiries, please WhatsApp us at +91 73586 50774 and we'll work out a custom shipping quote.
