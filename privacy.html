Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Elite Emporium ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit elite-emporium-one.vercel.app or place an order with us.

1. What we collect

  • Order details you provide at checkout: name, phone number, delivery address, PIN code, optional email and order notes.
  • Cart and wishlist data stored in your browser's local storage — never sent to us until you place an order.
  • Order history stored locally in your browser so you can view past orders without an account.
  • Anonymous usage data (page visits, scroll behavior) handled by your browser; we don't run third-party analytics on this site.

2. How we use it

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To contact you about your order via WhatsApp / phone / email.
  • To issue GST invoices when you request them.
  • To improve your shopping experience (e.g., "recently viewed", auto-fill from your last address).

3. Who we share it with

We share your details only with:

  • Courier partners who deliver your order (Delhivery, India Post, etc.).
  • Payment partners if you choose online payment (e.g. Razorpay — they have their own privacy policy).
  • Government authorities if legally required (e.g., GST audit).

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to anyone for marketing.

4. Cookies and storage

We use your browser's localStorage to remember your cart, wishlist, recent searches, and last-typed delivery details — purely to make your next visit smoother. You can clear this anytime via your browser's "Clear site data" option. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

5. Data retention

Order records are retained for as long as required by Indian tax law (typically 8 years for GST records). Other data lives only in your browser and is deleted when you clear it.

6. Your rights

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Withdraw consent for marketing messages at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, WhatsApp us at +91 73586 50774 or email eliteemporium112024@gmail.com.

7. Security

Payment data is handled entirely by our payment partners (Razorpay) over HTTPS — we never see your card number or UPI PIN. Our admin panel is protected by Firebase Authentication.

8. Children

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone in that age group.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. We'll never reduce your existing privacy rights without your consent.
